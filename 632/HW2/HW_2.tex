Let's look at $x_1$ first, since $V(x)$ is increasing there. The WKB approximation is
\[ \psi_\text{WKB}(x) = \begin{cases}
    \frac{A_<}{\sqrt{p(x)}}\sin\left[\frac{1}{\hbar}\cint{x}{x_1}p(x')\dd x'\right] + \frac{B_<}{\sqrt{p(x)}}\cos\left[\frac{1}{\hbar}\cint{x}{x_1}p(x')\dd x'\right]& x < x_1 \\
    \frac{A_F}{\sqrt{\abs{p(x)}}}\exp\left[-\frac{1}{\hbar}\cint{x_1}{x}\abs{p(x')}\dd x'\right] & x > x_1
\end{cases} \]
\[ \psi_\text{patch}(x) = a\text{Ai}(\nu x) + b\text{Bi}(\nu x), \quad \nu = \left(\frac{2mV'(x_1)}{\hbar^2}\right)^{\frac{1}{3}}. \]
To match them up, let's first consider $x \gtrsim x_1$. In this region
\[ \abs{p(x)} \approx \hbar\sqrt{\nu^3(x-x_1)}, \]
so our WKB solution for $x > x_1$ can approximately be written
\[ \psi_\text{WKB}(x) \approx \frac{A_F}{\sqrt{\hbar}\nu^{3/4}(x-x_1)^{1/4}}\exp\left[-\frac{2}{3}\nu^{3/2}(x-x_1)^{3/2}\right]. \]
To cast-off the unphysical asymptotic behaviour of $\text{Bi}(x)$, we set $b=0$. Using the asymptotic form of $\text{Ai}(x)$, we see that
\[ \psi_\text{patch}(x) \approx \frac{a}{\sqrt{\hbar}\nu^{1/4}(x-x_1)^{1/4}}\exp\left[-\frac{2}{3}\nu^{3/2}(x-x_1)^{3/2}\right]. \]
These expressions are equivalent provided
\[ a = \sqrt{\frac{4\pi}{\hbar\nu}}A_F. \]
