\section*{Exercise 1}
If 
\[ \vec{J} = J_x\hat{x} + J_y\hat{y} + J_z\hat{z} \]
Then
\begin{align*}
    \left(\vec{J}\cross\vec{J}\right)_\gamma & = J_\alpha J_\beta \epsilon_{\alpha\beta\gamma} \\
    & = J_\alpha J_\beta - J_\beta J_\alpha \\
    & = \comm{J_\alpha}{J_\beta} \\
    & = i\hbar J_\gamma
\end{align*}
Thus
\[ \vec{J}\cross\vec{J} = i\hbar\vec{J} \]
