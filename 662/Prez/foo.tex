\section*{Problem 4b}
Given
\[ \rho = \frac{1}{2}\left(\mathcal{I} + \vec{r}\cdot\vec{\sigma}\right), \quad \abs{\vec{r}} \leq 1, \]
show that
\[ \Tr[\rho^2] = \frac{1}{2}\left(1 + \abs{\vec{r}}^2\right). \]
Proof (typeset version of Ellie Schnee's work): \\
\begin{align*}
    \Tr[\rho^2] &= \Tr[\rho\rho] \\
    &= \frac{1}{2}\Tr[\rho\left(\mathcal{I} + \vec{r}\cdot\vec{\sigma}\right)] \\
    &= \frac{1}{2}\Tr[\rho] + \frac{1}{2}\Tr[\rho\left(\vec{r}\cdot\vec{\sigma}\right)] \\
    &= \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{2}\vec{r}\cdot\Tr[\rho\vec{\sigma}] \\
    &= \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{2}\vec{r}\cdot\langle\vec{\sigma}\rangle \\
    &= \frac{1}{2}\left(1 + \abs{\vec{r}}^2\right)
\end{align*}
